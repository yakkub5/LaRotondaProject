Dangerous animals

If you have a dog of these features now, or plan to in the future, you should know some responsibilities associated with this type of animal:

1. Obtaining license (which handles the municipality where you live). To apply you must be of age, have no criminal record, a certificate of fitness psicotécnico and liability insurance.

2. Identification of animal by microchip.

3. veterinary Cartilla with vaccinations.

4. Walking on the street: the dog should be taken only by the authorized person, always muzzled and go strapped 2m. maximum length.
Psicotécnico fitness certificate serves to prove that the person who is going to perform is able physically and psychologically to have an animal with aggressive potential.
